     Fix: Blank Space Below Services Section

     ProCraft Handyman Landing Page

     Context

     The horizontal service strip (item #6 from the previous implementation)
     creates a blank space immediately after the Services section on desktop.
     This is caused by GSAP's automatic pinSpacing behavior: when pin: true is
     set on .services__scroll-pin, GSAP inserts a spacer element after the
     pinned element to reserve scroll distance for the horizontal animation.
     This spacer has the same height as the scroll distance (typically
     1000вҖ“2500px depending on viewport) and appears as dead blank space with
     only the section background color visible.

     Root cause: The pin trigger is .services__scroll-pin (just the card
     container), not the full .services section. The section header sits above
     the pin trigger, outside of it. When GSAP creates the spacer, it adds it
     after .services__scroll-pin but still inside the .services section вҖ”
     between the cards and the next <section> element. The spacer has no content
      and the section background color fills it visually, making a large empty
     void.

     ---
     Critical Files

     - C:/Dev/handyman/main.js вҖ” initServicesHScroll() at line 817
     - C:/Dev/handyman/index.html вҖ” Services section HTML structure (~line 195)
     - C:/Dev/handyman/styles.css вҖ” .services__scroll-pin styles at ~line 2408

     slightly so both the section header and card grid are inside one pinned
     container. This way:
     1. GSAP pins the whole section (header + cards visible throughout the

     2. The spacer is placed after the .services section, in normal page flow вҖ”
     not creating visual dead space inside it
     3. The section header stays readable while cards scroll horizontally вҖ” a
