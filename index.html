20 Years of Craft.
One Standard: Done Right.

ProCraft Construction & Handyman was founded on a simple belief: people deserve a contractor they can actually trust. One who shows up, tells the truth about the job, charges a fair price, and takes pride in the work long after the tools are packed up.

Owner Mike Caldwell has been working trades in central Illinois since 2004. Whether it's a single broken door hinge or a full basement remodel, every job gets the same level of care — because your home deserves it.
